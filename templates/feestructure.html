Which place is best for studying MBBS abroad at low cost?

Every medical aspirant have the same question : which is the best place to study MBBS at Low Cost ? As compared to India MBBS in Abroad is less expensive in nature. You can get plenty of options for studying MBBS under budget of Rs. 20-30 lakhs. The most popular destinations for MBBS in Abroad are Kyrgyzstan, Kazakhstan, Philippines, Russia, Ukraine, China, and Bangladesh among Indian students. Indian Students are choosing Abroad Medical Universities because of its Global Recognition , Low Cost & good exposure. As they complete their medical studies from Abroad, wishing to practice in India must appear and clear the license exam.
Previously, students returning to India have to undergo FMGE i.e., Foreign Medical Graduate Examination. in order to acquire the license to practice as Doctor
But Now, National Exit Test will be the licensing exam introduced by the National Medical Commission Bill 2019
It is a single-window examination that is to be implemented by 2022. It is a mandatory examination for students completing MBBS from India and Abroad It will also set a standard for medical practitioners in India. The students from India as well as Abroad for holding the license to practice as doctors in the country have to appear for the NExT exam.
